History of Aankh ladi joi joi thaaki

Ankh ladi joi joi thaki is a ginan that leads us back to the time Pir sayad fazal shah who wrote this ginan for the mowla of that time

Hindi translation

अँख लाडी जोई जोकी एक गिन्नन है जो हमें उस समय तक वापस ले जाती है पीर सयाद फ़ज़ल शाह जो उस समय के इमाम के लिए इस जिनान को लिखते थे


Verse 1

ejee aa(n)khaladdi joi joi thaakee
kyaare aave moro shaah
partak jaannee paae paddun
vaalaa namee namee karu(n) parnnaam

Translation

My eyes have become tired continuously watching and waiting, as to when my Mawlaa will arrive. Having known him to be the Manifest, I may bow at His feet, O Beloved, I would pay my respects to You in a bowing manner.

Chorus/Verani

mitthaa shaah  samaru(n) me(n) tero naam
yaa shaah samaru(n) me(n) tero naam
khudaava(n)d  samaru(n) me(n)  tero naam

Samaru(n) me(n) tero, Samaru(n) me(n) tero naam, Mitthaa shaah samuru(n) me(n) tero naam

Translation

O my sweet Mawlaa! I remember your name. Oh Mawlaa! I remember your name. O God! I remember your name.


Verse 2

Eji Baal pannaani pritddi
saami tamsun laagiji;
nehddo laago Shaah noor dharse
so kiyaa jaanne gemaar - mitthaa Shaah samaru(n) 

Translation

My love since my childhood, O Lord, is for You. I am in love with the 'Nur' of the time(Hazar Imam). How can the ignorant know about it. O my sweet Mawlaa!...

Verse 3

Eji Sayyad Fazal Shaah Aakhe ventee
mujhse maher karo maherbaan
karam karoge to sharam rahengee
ham ba(n)do dosaaree tu taar...mitthaa shaah samaru(n) 

Translation

Sayyad Faazal Shah makes the supplication: O Merciful! have mercy upon me. If You shower Your mercy, my honor will be preserved, for I am a sinful creature and You are the Saviour. O my sweet Mawlaa!..
